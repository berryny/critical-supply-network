Critical Supply Network

A centralized inventory management system for all critical supplies

Our Mission

We aim to close the supply gaps that were exposed during the Covid 19 pandemic by offering affordable inventory tracking management for all suppliers, large and small and an online marketplace connecting buyers, such as government agencies, hospitals and schools to vetted manufacturers large and small

Benefits of Inventory Management

Better Inventory Accuracy: With solid inventory management, you know what's in stock and order only the amount of inventory you need to meet demand.

Reduced Risk of Overselling: Inventory management helps track what's in stock and what's on back order, so you don't oversell products.

Access for Small Manufacturers

Smaller manufacturers often miss out on the benefits of inventory management due to the high costs of technology implementation. We offer an affordable solution that enables these small and mid-size manufacturers to benefit from the analytics and insights that has previously only been available to larger companies.

Public and Private Sector in one place

The federal government maintains aa National Stockpile for critical supplies,, but fails to accurately assess the supply capabilities of the private sector. The Critical Supply Network gives an overview of critical supply levels.

How it Works

Sensors placed on machinery send real-time inventory data to the Critical Supply Network, which can be accessed by the manufacturer with data analytics and machine learning to make important insights about production quantities and industry demand.

A Marketplace of fairness and efficiency

Our vendors are vetted, to ensure products meet industry standards, which eliminates the risk of economic loss from buying counterfeit products. Real-time inventory levels help manufacturers adjust production levels, reducing waste or meeting demand. Our marketplace connects buyers and sellers using machine learning technology to recommend the best fit.
